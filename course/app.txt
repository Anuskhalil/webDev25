Web Development Topics to cover

### **1. HTML (Structure of the Web)**
- **Basics:** Elements, Tags, Attributes  
- **Page Structure:** `<!DOCTYPE html>`, `<html>`, `<head>`, `<body>`  
- **Text Elements:** Headings, Paragraphs, Lists  
- **Forms & Inputs:** `<form>`, `<input>`, `<label>`, `<button>`, validation  
- **Semantic HTML:** `<header>`, `<nav>`, `<section>`, `<article>`, `<footer>`  
- **Media Elements:** Images, Audio, Video  
- **Tables:** `<table>`, `<tr>`, `<td>`, `<th>`  
- **Links & Navigation:** Internal & External Links, Anchor Tags   


### **2. CSS (Styling the Web)**
- **Selectors & Specificity** (`class`, `id`, `element`, `pseudo-classes`)  
- **Box Model** (`margin`, `border`, `padding`, `width`, `height`)  
- **Positioning & Layouts** (`static`, `relative`, `absolute`, `fixed`, `sticky`)  
- **Flexbox** (Row/Column Alignment, Justify/Align Content)  
- **CSS Grid** (Grid Tracks, Areas, Templates)  
- **Colors & Backgrounds** (`background-image`, `gradient`, `opacity`)  
- **Typography & Fonts** (`font-family`, `font-size`, `text-align`)  
- **Transitions & Animations** (`@keyframes`, `transform`, `hover effects`)  
- **Media Queries & Responsive Design**  
- **CSS Frameworks** (Bootstrap, Tailwind Basics)  


### **3. JavaScript (Programming the Web)**
- **Basics:** Variables (`var`, `let`, `const`), Data Types  
- **Operators & Expressions** (`+`, `-`, `*`, `/`, `%`, `++`, `--`)  
- **Functions:** Declaration, Expression, Arrow Functions  
- **Control Flow:** `if-else`, `switch`, Loops (`for`, `while`, `do-while`)  
- **DOM Manipulation:** `getElementById`, `querySelector`, `innerHTML`, `style`  
- **Events & Event Listeners** (`click`, `mouseover`, `keydown`)  
- **ES6+ Features:** Destructuring, Spread/Rest Operators, Template Literals  
- **Array Methods:** `map()`, `filter()`, `reduce()`, `forEach()`  
- **Objects & JSON** (`localStorage`, API Calls)  
- **Async JavaScript:** `setTimeout()`, `setInterval()`, Promises, `async/await`  
- **Basic Fetch API & AJAX**  


### **4. Hands-On Projects (Practical Learning)**
- **HTML & CSS:** Personal Portfolio, Landing Page, Blog Layout  
- **JavaScript Basics:** To-Do App, Calculator, Color Picker  
- **DOM Manipulation:** Image Slider, Accordion, Modal Popup  
- **API Integration:** Weather App, Fetching Data from JSON Placeholder
